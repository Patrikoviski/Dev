Espresso

Espresso is the most popular and well-known type of coffee in Italy. Obtained by roasting and grinding the seeds of Coffea arabica and Coffea robusta, it is prepared by machine using a high-pressure percolation process of hot water.

Doppio Espresso

Doppio Espresso is a double shot of espresso made using double the amount of ground coffee in a larger filter basket. This yields 60 ml of drink, twice as much as a single shot.
