Dogs

Buddy

Breed: Golden Retriever

Age: 3 years

Golden Retrievers are known for their friendly and tolerant attitude. They make great family pets, are highly intelligent, and eager to please, which makes them easy to train.

Max

Breed: Beagle

Age: 2 years

Beagles are curious and friendly dogs. They are known for their excellent sense of smell and tracking instinct. Beagles are great with kids and make wonderful family pets.

Charlie

Breed: Bulldog

Age: 4 years

Bulldogs are gentle and affectionate dogs. They are known for their loose, wrinkled skin and distinctive pushed-in nose. Bulldogs are great companions and are very loyal to their families.

Cats

Whiskers

Breed: Persian

Age: 5 years

Persian cats are known for their long, luxurious fur and sweet personalities. They are gentle and affectionate, making them great companions.

Shadow

Breed: Siamese

Age: 3 years

Siamese cats are known for their striking blue almond-shaped eyes and short coat. They are vocal and social, often forming strong bonds with their owners.

Oliver

Breed: Maine Coon

Age: 4 years

Maine Coons are one of the largest domesticated cat breeds. They are known for their friendly and playful nature, as well as their tufted ears and bushy tails.
